h1. OpenCourseSearch.org

OpenCourseSearch.org is a project to free the data trapped inside aging university course databases. It was born out of frustration with how difficult searching for courses can be on these systems.

More info: http://opencoursesearch.org/about/

Schools:
  - New York University
  - Ohio State University
  - University of California, Los Angeles
  - University of Nebraska-Lincoln
  - University of Nebraska at Omaha
  - University of Nebraska Kearney

Requirements:
  - Django
  - Redis
